## SEO & Keyword Strategy

### Keyword Types to Target

1. **Long-tail keywords** — 3-5 words, specific to pain point
   - Example: "Bible verses for anxiety," "prayer when suffering," "grief at holidays"
   - Lower search volume than single keywords
   - Higher intent to purchase or engage

2. **Question keywords** — What people actually ask
   - Example: "How do I pray when anxious?" "What Bible verses help with grief?"
   - Use Answer the Public or Google Autocomplete to find these

3. **Seasonal keywords** — Time-specific spikes
   - Example: "Grief at the holidays," "New Year reset," "Back to school anxiety"

### Keyword Research Tools

- **Google Search Console** — See what searches bring traffic to your site
- **Google Trends** — Identify rising search topics
- **Answer the Public** — Visualize questions people ask (free limited version)
- **YouTube Autocomplete** — Type topic, see what people search for
- **Reddit/Christian forums** — Real customer language and questions
