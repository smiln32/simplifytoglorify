# Business Overview

## What We Sell

**Simplify to Glorify** sells Christian devotional PDFs organized around emotional and spiritual struggles.

### 5 Product Types
- **Journals** — Guided journaling with Scripture and prompts
- **Scripture Cards** — Printable/downloadable Scripture reference cards
- **Prayer Cards** — Prayer guide cards with specific focus areas
- **7-Day Resets** — Structured 7-day devotional/prayer/journaling experiences
- **Devotionals** — Shorter daily devotional content

### 14 Product Categories
Each category includes Scripture-anchored tools focused on specific struggles:

1. **ADHD** — Grace for how God made you; emotional steadiness
2. **Anxiety** — Calming fear; trusting God in unknowns
3. **Caregiving** — Walking alongside loved ones (Alzheimer's, dementia, hospice, spousal)
4. **Chronic Pain** — God's nearness in daily suffering
5. **Depression** — Heavy seasons; empty nest; job loss
6. **Gratitude** — Noticing God's goodness
7. **Grief** — Loss support (child, parent, sibling, spouse, stillborn)
8. **Loneliness** — Remembering you are seen and held
9. **Patience** — Trusting God's timing
10. **Peace** — Receiving peace that surpasses understanding
11. **Prayer** — Deepening conversation with God; unanswered prayer
12. **Regret** — Finding forgiveness; freedom from past
13. **Struggling with Faith** — For seasons when belief feels hard
14. **Teachers** — Grace and encouragement for educator burnout
15. **Uncertainty** — Trusting God with what you cannot control

See `src/data/products.ts` for full taxonomy with subtopics.

## Target Audience

### Demographics
- Primarily **Christian women**
- Spiritually earnest; looking for honest, Scripture-rooted resources
- Dealing with real emotional/spiritual challenges

### Primary Struggles
- Anxiety and fear
- Grief (especially complicated grief)
- ADHD/neurodivergence
- Caregiving burden and burnout
- Chronic pain/illness
- Faith crises (doubt, unanswered prayer)
- Loneliness and isolation

### What They Want
- Scripture-anchored, not fluffy inspiration
- Practical tools they can actually use
- Permission to acknowledge hard emotions
- Reminders that God is present in suffering
